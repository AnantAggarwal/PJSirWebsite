About PJ Sir

Prashant Jain, popularly known as PJ Sir, is one of India's most celebrated Mathematics educators. An alumnus of IIT Bombay, he secured All India Rank 42 in IIT JEE, is an INChO Gold Medalist, and a qualified INPhO scholar.

He is currently the National Outcomes Head at Unacademy and has been mentoring JEE aspirants for over a decade. With prior experience at renowned institutions like Resonance and Nucleus in Kota, PJ Sir has authored 18 books, including the highly acclaimed IOQM Mathematics series published by Pearson.

A passionate mentor, speaker, and content creator, PJ Sir is committed to guiding students through their competitive exam journey with clarity and confidence.
